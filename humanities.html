Class Description

Humanities is taught by Ms. Small and it explores the question, “What does it mean to be human?” Through readings, group discussions, and open-ended writing prompts, we study what caused humanity to change over time and how humans handle that change.

This was the second essay we wrote in B Term which we studied various rhetorical devices used in satire. For the essay, we were tasked with finding a satirical piece we could relate to and writing an analysis of it. I chose to compare a satirical and serious article covering Elon Musk.
