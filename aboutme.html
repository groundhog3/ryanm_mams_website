Introduction

Hello there once again! My name is Ryan Mechery and I’m a junior at MAMS. I was born in Worcester, MA and now live in Auburn. I previously went to Saint Paul’s Diocesan Jr./Sr. Highschool(formerly Holy Name) where I attended for four years – from seventh to tenth grade. In middle school I was involved with the Chorus program and Lego Mindstorms©. In high school, I was involved with Mock Trial, NHS, and served as my Class Treasurer.

Family

My sister and I were born here, but my parents were born in Kerala, India.

My dad is a software engineer at Hartford Financial Services Group, Inc., but I know him as a goofy person that tells really bad dad jokes. He always gives me good advice and really helped me with my transition to MAMS.

My mom is a nurse at UMASS Memorial, and she is the funniest and quirkiest person I know. I find it very easy to talk to her and she always encourages me when I am down.

My sister’s name is Andrea, and she is a junior at UCONN. Even though we may not get along all the time, she always sticks up for me and she’s the best (and only) sister I know. She is also an alumnus of the Mass Academy class of 2019, so I've been getting a lot of tips about life at MAMS.

All in all, my family has always been supportive and believed in me more than I did myself. Even when I applied to MAMS, they told me they were proud of my accomplishments, regardless of the results. For that, I can never thank them enough. With that being said, I encourage all the students reading my website to thank your parents and siblings for all their hard work and the trust they've confided within you.

Community Service

I'm the Junior Treasurer of a non-profit called the Kerala Association of New England. As Treasurer, I would normally be tasked with coordinating funds for fundraisers and bake sales. However, due to less in-person events in 2021 overall, that task was delegated to the organization's board for online transactions. The Junior committee, which is comprised of other highschool students, has helped organize two main events so far which were a virtual Spelling Bee and virtual Halloween event open to all children.
