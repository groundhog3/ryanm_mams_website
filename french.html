Class Description

French is taught by Mme. Wildfong and it’s a fully immersive class in which we only speak the language full-time. Through weekly listening journals, watching movies, listening to songs, and playing French games, we can expand our vocabulary and try new things in a low-stress environment.

Cooking Project

In B Term, one of our assignments was to pick a cuisine from a French-speaking country, cook it for the class, and create a presentation on the process. I chose to make des champignon farcis or stuffed mushrooms.
